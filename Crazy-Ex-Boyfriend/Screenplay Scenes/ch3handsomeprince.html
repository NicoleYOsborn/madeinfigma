FADE IN

INT. ENCHANTED FOREST - DAY

(Animated in the style of classic Disney films)

The sun-dappled forest glows with an otherworldly beauty. Prince Remus Lupin, a handsome but sheltered young man with flowing locks, wears a royal cape as he picks berries, accompanied by his faithful dog, Padfoot. A longing fills his eyes as he gazes beyond the village.

PRINCE REMUS

(whispering to Padfoot)

Padfoot, I know there is more to life than this. Some days I wish someone would just come and steal me away.

Padfoot looks up at Remus with big, concerned puppy eyes, sensing his restlessness.

PRINCE REMUS

Oh, don't look at me like that. Of course, I would never do anything to put myself in danger.

Remus continues to pick berries, humming a soft tune. Padfoot's unease grows, and he starts whining, his hair standing on end. He grips Remus's cape with his teeth and tugs.

PRINCE REMUS

(angry)

Padfoot! What has gotten into you? Oh!

He looks up and gasps, seeing themselves surrounded by a pack of menacing wolves, eyes gleaming yellow and teeth bared. The basket of berries falls to the ground as Remus tells Padfoot to run. They scramble through the woods in a panic and burst out onto a road, coming to an abrupt halt.

Before them, a company of gallant Knights on horseback blocks the road. The Prince and Padfoot are startled.

SIR BLACK

(from the head of the party, handsome and valiant)

Woah! Who goes there?

Prince Remus steps forward, panting and out of breath.

PRINCE REMUS

Wolves! In the forest!

The Knights turn their gaze toward the forest, witnessing the wolf pack skitter to a stop and retreat upon the sight of these valiant men on magnificent steeds.

SIR BLACK

(earnestly)

We seem to have found you just in time. You should know that these woods are unsafe to be traveling alone. I am Sir Black, and these are my knights, Sir James and Sir Peter. Please, allow us to escort you back to the village safely.

Sir Black extends a hand to help the Prince onto his horse, their eyes meeting in a moment of enchantment.

PRINCE REMUS

(flabbergasted)

I'm Remus, I'm… er… a baker. In the village. I've never ridden on a horse before.

Sir Black smiles warmly, their connection undeniable.

SIR BLACK

(encouraging)

Don't worry, Remus. You're safe with me. Do you trust me?

Remus takes Sir Black's hand, and the journey into the unknown world beyond the village begins, filled with magic, adventure, and the promise of true love.
